Beef Wellington Recipe

omg how do you even make that! We'll show you. It may look impossible but expert chefs have been able to make it before so why not give it a shot.

Ingredients

  • Beef(leg maybe)
  • Buy some pastry
  • mushrooms
  • Gravy
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Roll out pastry
  2. Mush up mushrooms and fry them in some butter
  3. Put oven on very hot
  4. Coat beef leg in mushed mushrooms
  5. Roll the beef coated in mushed mushrooms in the pastry then whack it in the oven
  6. Cook for 5-6 hours?
  7. Serve when pink in middle
