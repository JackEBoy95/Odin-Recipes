pancakes Recipe

Mmm lovely fluffy pancakes. put butter on them if you dare. Yum yum yum!

Ingerdients

  • Flour 500g
  • Sugar - as much as you like
  • Some butter
  • Milk - 2 litres

Steps

  1. Mix Flour and Sugar
  2. Mix in the milk
  3. Once batter is together get a frying pan and melt some butter on medium heat
  4. Once butter is melted pour some batter into pan and cook on one side for ten minutes
  5. once cooked opn one side flip and do the same on the other
  6. Empty cooked pancake onto plate and repeat steps again until all batter is used
