Lasagna Recipe

lasagna for eating. if youre hungry come and get this tasty dish complete with everything that makes lasagna, lasagna. Eat and say cheese! You'll be coming back for more and more:]

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • cheese
  • Basil
  • Tomatoe
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 800
  2. Pour oil into pan and let heat for 5 minutes
  3. Throw lasagna sheets along with salt & Pepper into pan and fry at high heat for 20 minutes
  4. While that is happening, throw cheese into microwave and heat until liquid
  5. Baste tomatoes with liquid cheese. Add to frying pan
  6. Place contents into a baking tin and top with basil
  7. Cook for 5 minutes then withdraw. Place a knife through the centre. If knife is melted into dish, serve.
