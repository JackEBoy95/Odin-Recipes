Chicken Curry Recipe

This chicken curry is the yummiest thing you'll put in your mouth until you try our lasagna. This chicken curry will literally blow you.

Ingedients

  • 2lbs of chicken Brests
  • 200g of rice
  • some curry seasoning
  • oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Some Corriandre for colour

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into some pieces
  2. Heat oil in pan
  3. Boil the rice
  4. Fry chicken for 3 minutes
  5. Add seasonings
  6. Add the green when you can then serve
